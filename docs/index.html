There are 10 types of people… those who understand binary and those who don’t.

Fundamentally, with technology we build tools, but it is people using those tools that is how problems are solved.

When it comes to building tools, sometimes there is an opportunity to build a tool that works down at a very low level where computing meets the machine. Likewise, at a very high level, we can use the strangeness of math to solve the otherwise impossible.

Most work occurs between these extremes. Here persistence, diligence and practice are the ways to get things done.
